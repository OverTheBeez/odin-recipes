Tater Tot Hotdish

Prep: 10min Cook: 70min Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 bag Ore-Ida™ Tater Tots™ frozen potatoes
  • 1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can green beans, drained
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350° F.
  2. 2 Brown beef, drain
  3. 3 Mix browned beef, salt, pepper, soup and green beans
  4. Pour in casserole
  5. 4 Place Tater Tots™ on top of beef mixture
  6. Then top off with cheddar cheese
  7. 5 Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes
  8. 5 Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes
  9. Remove foil and bake 15 minutes
  10. Serve immediately
