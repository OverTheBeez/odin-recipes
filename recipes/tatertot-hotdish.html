Tater Tot Hotdish

Prep: 10min Cook: 70min Servings: 8 1 1/2 lbs ground beef 1 bag Ore-Ida™ Tater Tots™ frozen potatoes 1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup 1 can green beans, drained 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon pepper Preheat oven to 350° F. 2 Brown beef, drain. 3 Mix browned beef, salt, pepper, soup and green beans. Pour in casserole. 4 Place Tater Tots™ on top of beef mixture. Then top off with cheddar cheese. 5 Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15 minutes. 6 Serve immediately.
